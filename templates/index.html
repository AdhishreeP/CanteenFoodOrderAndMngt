Order in few easy steps

Create your account

Click on Sign-Up to create your account. Enter your information like name, email and password and your account will get created.

Login with your details

Once you create an account, log in with your credentials like email and password to see the entire menu of the canteen.

Order in a few minutes

After you log in, you can see the wide variety of menu available in the canteen. Select from the menu and place your order by paying online or at the cash counter.
